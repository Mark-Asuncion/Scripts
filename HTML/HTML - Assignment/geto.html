Suguru Geto is an antagonist in Jujutsu Kaisen. He is a Special Grade Sorcerer who specializes on collecting and controlling cursed spirits. He was a classmate of Satoru Gojo and Shoko Ieiri at Tokyo Metropolitan Curse Technical College and a former student of Masamichi Yaga.
