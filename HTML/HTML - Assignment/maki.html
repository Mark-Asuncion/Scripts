Maki Zenin is a major supporting character in Jujutsu Kaisen and one of the main protagonists of the Prequel Series. She is currently is a second-year at Tokyo Metropolitan Curse Technical College.

Originally from the Zenin Family, Maki left on her own due to being rejected for her lack of Cursed Energy. She studies to become a strong Sorcerer despite her own family working against her.
